What we do


Dog walking services

Walking the dog regularly provides a basic foundation for physical and mental health. Like a child, your dog wants to know the world. If he or she is confined to the house for too long, your dog will get bored, and boredom can lead to destructive behavior.

Dog grooming services

We are invest in hygienic care and cleaning of a dog as well as enhancing a dog's physical appearance for showing or other types of competitions.

Excerise and training services

We engage dogs in various excerises to keep them in top physical conditions since daily exercises maintain their fitness, immunity, and overall health.

Training services are also availbale to enhance communication between the owner and the dog.

Pet sitting Services

We are here to sort you out. With us you can create time for your daily activities as we take care of your dog.

We also offer you chance to call us and we pick the dog and deliver it back to you once your satisfied by our services.

We at Petrite Bay love pets just as you and our main goal is to serve your pet with integrity and care for they play a big role in our lives.
